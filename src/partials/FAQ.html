  • Who is the best ecologist in the world?

    Aldo Leopold
    Considered the father of wildlife ecology, Leopold was a pioneer in the field of conservation biology. His book "A Sand County Almanac" is considered a classic of environmental literature.

  • What do we do in ecology?

    Ecology is the study of the relationships between living organisms, including humans, and their physical environment; it seeks to understand the vital connections between plants and animals and the world around them.
